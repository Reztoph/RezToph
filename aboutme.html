Me

Hello, I am RezToph, an independent music producer. My philosophy regarding music production is rooted in the idea of open access and creative freedom. Consequently, I release my work to the public free of charge. I encourage the use and distribution of my compositions across various media and creative projects; my primary concern is not the specific nature of how the music is utilized, but rather the preservation of my creative identity.

To facilitate this while protecting my work from unauthorized claims of ownership, I am implementing a formal licensing structure for my catalog. This decision is not motivated by financial gain, but by a necessity to ensure that my name—RezToph—remains permanently and accurately associated with my original compositions.

While I am happy to provide this resource to the creative community, I am aware that certain individuals may attempt to misappropriate my work or present it as their own. By licensing these tracks, I am establishing a clear legal framework that guarantees my right to attribution. This ensures that any time my music is featured, broadcast, or integrated into a new project, I am recognized as the sole creator.

As long as you provide clear and visible credit to RezToph, you have my full support in using my music. This professional standard helps maintain a respectful environment for independent creators and prevents the unauthorized "theft" of intellectual property.
